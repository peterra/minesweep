How to Play Minesweeper

Goal

Reveal every cell on the board that doesn't contain a mine. If you click a mine, the game is over.

Controls

  • Left click — Reveal a cell
  • Right click — Place or remove a flag on a cell you suspect is a mine

Numbers

When you reveal a cell, it shows a number indicating how many of its 8 neighboring cells contain mines:

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8

If a cell has no adjacent mines, it appears blank and all its neighbors are automatically revealed.

Winning

You win when every non-mine cell has been revealed. You don't need to flag all the mines — just avoid clicking them.

Settings

  • Rows / Cols — Change the board size (5–30)
  • Mines — Set the number of mines (capped to fit the board)
  • Upload Background — Add a custom background image behind the board

Tips

  • Start by clicking near the center — corners are more likely to give small reveals.
  • Use flags to keep track of mines you've identified through logic.
  • If a numbered cell already has that many flagged neighbors, the remaining neighbors are safe.
